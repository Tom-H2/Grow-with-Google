Peregrin Falcon

The Peregrin Falcon is known as the fastest animal alive with a reported speed in a stoop at more than 200 mph!

  • Scientific Name: Falco pereginus
  • Average Wingspan: 40 inches
  • Average Height: 16 inches
  • Habitat: Rocky ledges, cliffs, and tall buildings.

Taken to the brink of extinction due to thinning egg shells caused by DDT, Peregrin falcons have made a remarkable recovery in recent decades. These raptors hunt almost exclusively by diving at their prey and hitting them in mid-air. Their pointed wings and long narrow tail distinguishes them from most other hawks as does their distinctive rounded dark bar below the eyes.
